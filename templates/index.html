Extraction

Extraction techniques retrieve key sentences from the original text. These techniques can involve algorithms that analyze document structure, such as sentence importance, or more sophisticated approaches using natural language processing (NLP) to extract relevant concepts and facts.

TF-IDF

TF-IDF calculates the importance of words in a document based on their frequency. It helps identify terms that are most relevant to a document, making it a crucial technique for information retrieval, document clustering, and text mining.

SVD

Singular Value Decomposition (SVD) is a technique in linear algebra used for dimensionality reduction, especially in large datasets. It is also applied in topic modeling and matrix factorization for collaborative filtering in recommendation systems.

Summarization

Text summarization condenses information while preserving its meaning. Techniques like extractive and abstractive summarization are used to generate concise versions of lengthy documents, making them more accessible for quick understanding.
